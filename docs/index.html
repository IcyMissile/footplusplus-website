A cheap FES device for footdrop treatment

Foot++ is developing a cheap Functional Electrical Stimulation (FES) device for treatment of footdrop (PENDING FDA 510K APPROVAL). We set out to create an accessible, affordable, and customizable version of an FES device for footdrop as an alternative to the extremely expensive devices available on the market right now.
